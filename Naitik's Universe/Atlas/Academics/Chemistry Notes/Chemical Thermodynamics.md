==Thermodynamics is the study of heat, work, energy, and the relationships between them during physical and chemical processes.==\
1. **System**: A part of the Universe under the thermodynamic investigation is called the **system**.
2. **Surroundings**: The Universe outside the system is called the **surroundings**.
3. **Boundary**: Anything which separates System from Surroundings is called as **Boundary**.



4. Isothermal process : The process in which the 1. the change in Temperature is constant, ΔT=0.
                                          2.Heat is exchanged with surroundings.
                                          3.Volume and Pressure are Variable
    For an ideal gas, in isothermal process, ΔE=0(Internal Energy), ΔH=0(Enthalpy)
